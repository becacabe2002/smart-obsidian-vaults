# Perplexity Research Prompt

## Goal
To conduct deep, structured research on a specific topic using Perplexity AI, ensuring high-quality sources and synthesized insights.

## Prompt
### Prompt 1 - Literature Review
```text
Act as a professional academic research assistant.
Finad and synthesis the most highly cited scientific studies from the last 5-10 years related to the topic: [TOPIC].
For each study, present:
* Author(s)
* Year of publication
* Research objective(s)
* Research methodology
* Key findings
* Contribution(s) of the study

Then, synthesize all of them into a coherent Literature Review Paragraph using an academic tone.
```

### Prompt 2 - Identifying Research Gaps
```text
Based on recent studies on the topic: [TOPIC],
Identify the research gaps, including:
1. Issues where previous studies have not reached an unified conclusion
2. Under-researched aspects or perspectives
3. Unresearched target groups or contexts

Clearly present:
* The research gap
* Why this gap is important
* How a new study can address this gap
```

### Prompt 3 - Proposing Research methodologies
```text
For the research topic: [TOPIC],
Propose the 3 most suitable research methodologies.
For each methodology, present:
1. Nature of the methodology (qualitative, quantitative, mixed methods)
2. Implementation process
3. Type of data to collect
4. Data analysis method
5. Advantages
6. Disadvantages
7. Real-world research examples that have used this methodology.
```

### Prompt 4 - Creating Academic Citations (APA)
```text
Create a standard APA 7 citation list for the following sources:
[LIST OF SOURCES]
Requirements:
* Correct APA 7 formatting
* Clearly categorize: journal article/book/website
* Include DOI or URL if available
* Verify the accuracy of author and year information

Present this as a list that can be copied directly into a research paper.
```

### Prompt 5 - Writing a Thesis Statement
```text
Develop a clear thesis statement for a research paper on the topic: [TOPIC]
Requirements:
* Demonstrate the research stance
* Arguable and verifiable
* Suitable for academic writing style
* Provide direction for the entire research paper

Then explain:
* The significance of the thesis statement
* The research value of the thesis statement
```

### Prompt 6 - Structuring and academic essay
```text
Develop a detailed structured/outline for an academic essay on the topic: [TOPIC]
Include:
1. Introduction
  * Background
  * Research problem
  * Thesis statement

2. Main sections
  * Key arguments
  * Supporting studies
  * Analysis

3. Conclusion
  * Summary of findings
  * Research implication
  * Future research directions

Present this as a logical outline that can be developed into a complete paper.
```

### Prompt 7 - Proposing Research Hypothesis
```text
For the topic: [TOPIC]
Propose 1-2 testable research hypotheses
For each Hypothesis, present:
* Independent variable 
* Depedent variable
* Predicted relationship
* How to test the hypothesis
* Data collection method
```

### Prompt 8 - Finding Research Data Sources
```text
For research topic: [TOPIC],
Recommended reliable data sources.
For each source, state:
* Dataset name
* Providing organization
* Type of data (qualitative/quantitative)
* Data content
* Access link

Prioritize the following sources:
* Acadeic datasets
* Government data
* Research databases
```

### Prompt 9 - 10-Minute Presentation Outline
```text
Create an outline for a 10-minute academic presentation on the topic: [TOPIC]
Structure:
1. Opening (1–2 minutes)
2. Main content (6–7 minutes)
3. Conclusion (1–2 minutes)

Suggest:
* Number of slides
* Main content of each slide
* Image/chart suggestions
* Key takeaway message
```

### Prompt 10 - Comparing Research Perspectives
```text
Compare different research perspectives on the topic: [TOPIC]
Based on the following sources: 
[RESEARCH LINKS]
Analyse:
* The main perspective of each study
* Similarities
* Differences
* Supporting or counter-arguments
* Which study has the greatest impact
```

## Workflow
1. **Initial Search:** Run the prompt in Perplexity Pro.
2. **Refinement:** Use follow-up questions to drill down into specific citations.
3. **Capture:** Copy relevant snippets or the full synthesis into this vault.
